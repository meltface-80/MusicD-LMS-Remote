MusicD

MusicD LMS Remote

A music-discovery PWA and full-screen wall display for Lyrion Music Server — random-album browsing, instant whole-library search, rich label & artist pages, and effortless playback.

v1.0.10 Docker MIT licence Works over the network — no LMS plugin needed

Features

Everything runs against your existing LMS over HTTP — the app never touches your library files (except an optional read-only mount for label-tag scanning).

🎵Album discovery

Random album selection with filtering, Album of the day, Label of the week, Play Unheard, and recently-unplayed recommendations. The database learns what you've heard and resurfaces forgotten albums.

📚Rich library browsing

Browse by album, artist, genre, record label or decade — and jump between related artists, albums and labels from anywhere. Every artist credit is a tappable link, including each member of multi-artist billings.

🎤Artist pages

Artist photo, biography and the library's albums by that artist — bands list their members and musicians list their bands, all linked, powered by the LMS Music & Artist Information plugin, Qobuz and MusicBrainz.

🔍Powerful search

Instant whole-library search by album, artist or record label, with optional extension to Qobuz and TIDAL.

💿Detailed album pages

High-resolution artwork, track listing, release year, label, duration, multiple-artist support — plus album reviews from the LMS Music & Artist Information plugin or Qobuz, and Pitchfork scores with links where available.

📺Full-screen wall display

Turn a TV or tablet into a now-playing display: big artwork, artist photography, biographies, reviews, related albums and YouTube music videos with automatic rotation.

🏷Record label explorer

Label of the week, label artwork via Discogs and FanArt.tv, label merging with undo, and every release from a selected label.

📻Don't Stop The Music

When the queue finishes, LMS keeps the music flowing — pick any Don't Stop The Music provider per player, right from the app's settings.

▶Playback integration

Play or queue albums and tracks, multi-select, zone switching, Roon-style volume control, and a live queue view of what's still to come.

⚙️Settings, without leaving the app

Native per-player settings (rename, crossfade, replay gain, sync, volume mode) and the full LMS server settings embedded in the app.

🔄Automatic updates

Built-in update checker with one-click updates straight from GitHub.

Install with Docker

Pick where you're running the app, fill in the details, and copy the generated command. The app talks to LMS purely over the network (HTTP on the LMS web port, default 9000) — it does not need to run on the same machine as LMS.
